01 / Concept

A Scene Prepared for
Cuisine and Silence.

Scène means 'Scene' in French.

The weather, temperature, and scent of the air.
Ingredients from the fields and sea.
Expressing all of these as a 'scene on a plate'.

Away from the hustle and bustle, at the foot of Mt. Daisen,
we offer time to simply face the cuisine.

About the Name "Scène"

We harvest vegetables and herbs from our fields daily, so the varieties change day by day. The bounty of the sea also varies daily depending on the weather and fishing conditions. We offer courses that allow you to feel such scenes of the San-in region through our cuisine.

Auberge in the forest, Daisen, Tottori.
02 / Cuisine

The Blessings of San-in,
Through French Techniques.

We use ingredients from the San-in region.
Vegetables, fish, meat. We assemble the course facing the season.
We build the unique 'scene' of the land on the plate.

*Our cuisine is created by young chefs. They are refining their skills and sensibilities while facing the ingredients of this land.

03 / Guest Rooms

Guest rooms at Scène are designed for savoring the afterglow of dinner and the silence of Daisen.

05 / FAQ

We value a quiet environment for adults.

Can children stay?+
We accept guests aged 9 and over. We do not provide meals or facilities for small children to maintain a quiet environment.
Is it okay to be noisy?+
Scène is an accommodation premised on a quiet environment. We do not envision lively ways of spending time.
What is the meal like?+
Dinner is a course meal featuring ingredients from the San-in region. The content changes depending on the season and the situation of the day.
Do you accommodate food allergies?+
Yes, please contact us in advance if possible. We may not be able to accommodate depending on the content.
Please tell me the cancellation policy.+
Please check the cancellation policy on the reservation screen when booking.
