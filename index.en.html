01 / Concept

A single scene,
prepared for silence and cuisine.

"Scène" means "scene" or "setting" in French.

The weather of the day, the temperature, the scent of the air.
Ingredients that arrive from the fields and the sea, unique to that day.
To express all of these as a "scene on a plate".

At the foot of Mt. Daisen, far from the hustle and bustle of the city,
we have prepared a time for you to simply face the cuisine.

The Name "Scène"

Since we harvest vegetables and herbs from the field every day, the types of vegetables change daily. The blessings of the sea also change depending on the weather and fishing conditions. We offer a course where you can sense the ever-changing "scene" of the San'in region through our dishes.

Auberge in the forest of Daisen, Tottori.
02 / Cuisine

The blessings of San'in,
through French techniques.

The ingredients used are from the San'in region at that moment.
Vegetables, fish, meat. We assemble the day's course while facing the season.
We create a "Scène" of the land—where no two days are the same—upon the plate.

*The cuisine is created by Hotaka Maeda, a chef from Tottori Prefecture. While working as a head chef at a bistro in Tokyo, he also supported the kitchen as an assistant at a Michelin-starred restaurant, learning fundamental techniques and the attitude towards the craft. Considering his cooking journey still in progress, he continues to refine his skills, aiming for a better dish by facing the local ingredients and the materials themselves.

03 / Guest Rooms

Enjoying the margins,
a space of silence.

The guest rooms at Scène are places to immerse yourself in the afterglow of the meal and the silence of Daisen.
We have not provided excessive decorations or facilities,
but rather prepared a view of the forest framed by the window and a margin where you can take a deep breath.
It is a space solely for sleeping peacefully.

05 / FAQ

We cherish being a place where adults can spend time quietly.

Can children stay?+
Dining is limited to guests aged 9 and older. To value the quiet environment, we do not provide meals or facilities for small children.
What kind of meal is served?+
Dinner is a course meal featuring ingredients from the San'in region. The content changes depending on the season and the situation of the day.
Is allergy support available?+
If you let us know in advance, we will respond as much as possible.
