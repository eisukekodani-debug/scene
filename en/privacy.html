Privacy Policy

Last updated · 2026.05.09

Scène (hereafter "the restaurant"), operated by NPO Yui (NPO法人 結), establishes the following privacy policy regarding the handling of personal information on its official website scene-daisen.com (hereafter "this site").

01 / Information

Information collected

This site may collect the following information:

  • Page views, referrer, device information, time on site, and similar data collected via cookies and similar technologies
  • Name, email address, and inquiry content provided through the contact form
02 / Analytics

Use of analytics tools

This site uses Google Analytics 4 provided by Google LLC for site improvement purposes. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect traffic data; IP addresses are anonymized (anonymize_ip) and Google Signals is disabled.

Collected data is used solely to analyze site usage and is not used to identify individuals. To opt out of Google's data collection, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

03 / Cookies

Cookies and consent

This site displays a banner requesting consent to cookie use on the first visit. If you choose "Decline", Google Analytics will not track your activity. Consent status is saved in your browser's local storage and can be cleared anytime via browser settings.

04 / Purpose

Purpose of use

Information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Understanding site usage and improving services
  • Responding to inquiries and reservation-related communication
  • Compliance with applicable laws
05 / Disclosure

Third-party disclosure

NPO Yui will not provide collected personal information to third parties except with the consent of the individual or as required by law. Reservation-related information may be shared with reservation platform operators (Jalan, Booking.com, TableCheck, etc.).

06 / Rights

Disclosure, correction, and deletion requests

Upon request from the individual concerned for disclosure, correction, suspension of use, or deletion of held personal data, we will respond promptly within reasonable scope after verifying identity. Please contact us via the contact form on this site.

07 / Revisions

Revisions

This policy may be revised in response to changes in laws and regulations. Revised policies take effect from the time they are posted on this page.
