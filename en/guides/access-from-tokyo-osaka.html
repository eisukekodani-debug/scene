Getting to Scène from Tokyo,
Osaka & Overseas

All Routes  ·  2026 Edition

Scène is located at 大山 115-7, Daisen-cho, Saihaku-gun, Tottori Prefecture — approximately 600 m above sea level on the northern flank of Mt. Daisen 大山. The nearest major airport is Yonago Kitaro Airport 米子鬼太郎空港 (IATA: YGJ), from which Scène is a 40-minute drive. From Tokyo Haneda, the flight takes approximately 70 minutes; from Osaka Itami, approximately 50 minutes. This guide covers all practical routes by air, rail, and car from major Japanese cities and international gateways.

01 / From Tokyo

From Tokyo — Fastest Route: Haneda to Yonago by Air

A

Haneda (HND) → Yonago Kitaro Airport (YGJ) → Scène Recommended

Total travel time: approx. 2.5–3 hours (airport to Scène)

  • →Fly ANA or JAL from Tokyo Haneda Airport (HND) to Yonago Kitaro Airport (YGJ). Flight time: approx. 70 minutes. Frequency: 4–6 daily flights depending on season. Fares: from approx. ¥10,000–20,000 (advance purchase); standard fare approx. ¥35,000.
  • →Pick up a rental car at Yonago Kitaro Airport. All major rental companies have counters in the arrival hall (Toyota, Nissan, Orix, etc.). Drive approx. 40 km to Scène via National Route 431 and Prefectural Route 158. Free parking at Scène.
B

Shinkansen Tokyo → Okayama + Limited Express Yakumo → Yonago

Total travel time: approx. 6–6.5 hours

  • →Board the Nozomi Shinkansen from Tokyo Station to Okayama Station (approx. 3h20m).
  • →Transfer to JR Limited Express Yakumo at Okayama. Journey to Yonago Station: approx. 2h10m (8 round trips daily). Covered by the Japan Rail Pass (reserved seats required).
  • →Pick up a rental car at Yonago Station (multiple companies within walking distance) and drive approx. 40 km to Scène (40 min).
  • →Approximate fares: Shinkansen unreserved + Yakumo unreserved ≈ ¥22,000–25,000. The new Series 273 Yakumo (launched 2024) features active tilt control — significantly more comfortable than its predecessor.
02 / From Osaka

From Osaka / Kyoto / Kobe

A

Osaka Itami (ITM) → Yonago Kitaro Airport (YGJ) → Scène Fastest

Total travel time: approx. 2.5 hours (including airport procedures)

  • →ANA operates 2–3 daily flights from Osaka Itami (ITM) to Yonago Kitaro Airport (YGJ). Flight time: approx. 50 minutes. Fares from approx. ¥9,000 (advance purchase).
  • →Rental car from airport to Scène: approx. 40 min.
B

Shin-Osaka → Okayama (Shinkansen) + Yakumo → Yonago → Scène

Total travel time: approx. 3.5–4 hours

  • →Shinkansen from Shin-Osaka to Okayama: approx. 45–60 min (Nozomi or Hikari).
  • →Limited Express Yakumo from Okayama to Yonago: approx. 2h10m.
  • →Rental car from Yonago Station to Scène: approx. 40 min. Total fares approx. ¥15,000–20,000.

Highway Bus from Osaka

Highway buses operated by Nihon Kotsu and Ichibata Bus run between Namba/Umeda (Osaka) and Yonago. Journey time: approx. 4 hours (daytime) or 5 hours (overnight). Fares: approx. ¥3,500–5,500, making this the most budget-friendly option. Buses arrive at Yonago Bus Terminal (adjacent to Yonago Station); continue by rental car or taxi to Scène.

03 / From the Airport

From Yonago Kitaro Airport to Scène — Detailed Options

Yonago Kitaro Airport 米子鬼太郎空港 (officially Yonago Airport, IATA: YGJ) is located in Sakaiminato City, Tottori Prefecture, and has a 2,500 m runway. Domestic routes serve Haneda, Itami, and Naha. The airport holds an international terminal capable of receiving charter and seasonal international flights (see section 05 below).

Option Travel Time Approximate Cost Notes
Rental car (airport → Scène) Approx. 40 min ¥3,000–8,000 / day Strongly recommended — the Daisen area has limited public transport
Taxi (airport → Scène) Approx. 40 min ¥8,000–11,000 Available at the airport taxi rank; advance booking recommended in peak season
Bus (airport → Yonago Stn) + taxi Approx. 1.5 hours Bus ¥560 + taxi ¥9,000 Buses run 1–2 per hour; transfer to taxi at Yonago Station
A Note on Transport

Public transport options around Mt. Daisen are limited. We strongly recommend arranging a rental car at either Yonago Kitaro Airport or Yonago Station — this also allows you to explore nearby attractions including Daisenji Temple 大山寺, Mitsu Village 御机集落, Masumizu Highland 桝水高原, and Sakaiminato 境港 at your own pace. If you prefer not to drive, please enquire with us about private transfer arrangements.

04 / By Bus

Bus from Yonago Station to Daisen

Nihon Kotsu Bus runs a route from Yonago Station Bus Terminal (Platform 4) to Daisenji 大山寺, the stop closest to Scène. Journey time: approx. 50 minutes. Fare: approx. ¥920 one way. From the Daisenji bus stop, Scène is a 15-minute uphill walk (approx. 1 km). Services run 4–6 times daily (schedule varies by season; extra services operate during peak autumn-foliage and summer periods). Always confirm timetables in advance.

05 / International

From Hong Kong & Taiwan — International Routes

From Hong Kong (HKG)

No scheduled direct service between Hong Kong and Yonago currently operates year-round, though charter flights have operated seasonally in the past. The most practical route in 2026 is Hong Kong International Airport (HKG) → Kansai International Airport (KIX) or Osaka Itami (ITM) — multiple airlines operate direct flights (approx. 3h30m) — then connect to Yonago Kitaro Airport (YGJ) via ANA (approx. 50 min). Door-to-door from Hong Kong airport to Scène is typically 6–7 hours including connections.

Alternatively, fly into Okayama Airport (OKJ) from Hong Kong (some direct charter services have operated) and take the JR Limited Express Yakumo directly to Yonago (2h10m), then a rental car to Scène.

From Taiwan (Taipei / Kaohsiung)

China Airlines, EVA Air, and Peach Aviation operate multiple daily direct flights from Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE) to Osaka Kansai (KIX) or Itami (ITM) (approx. 2h30m). From Osaka, proceed to Yonago by the Itami–Yonago domestic flight (50 min) or by Shinkansen to Okayama + Yakumo (approx. 3h total). Direct flights from TPE to Okayama Airport (OKJ) have also been operated, bringing total travel time from Taipei to Scène to approximately 5–6 hours.

Seasonal charter flights between Taipei (TPE) and Yonago (YGJ) have operated previously, reducing total travel time to approximately 4 hours. Watch Yonago Airport's official website for announcements on international route resumptions.

Tips for International Visitors

The Japan Rail Pass covers all JR trains including the Limited Express Yakumo 特急やくも (reserved seat supplement required). Driving in Japan requires an International Driving Permit issued under the Geneva Convention. Rental car desks at Yonago Kitaro Airport and Yonago Station have English-language staff and some have Chinese-language support. Navigation apps (Google Maps, Yahoo! カーナビ) work well throughout the Daisen area.
